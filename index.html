About Us

We Are DebSoc

The IIEST Shibpur Debating Society (DebSoc) is one of the oldest and most prestigious societies on campus. We are not just about arguing; we are about building structured thought, fostering critical analysis, and developing confident public speakers.

Our members compete in national and international tournaments, host workshops, and engage in rigorous intellectual discussions. Whether you're a seasoned orator or have never spoken at a podium, DebSoc is the place to hone your skills.
